

The folder contains: 4 image files of the green, blue, grey and total water footprint 
	related to consumption in m3/cap/yr (Fig 10 of Report 50)


the image files:
- WF_perCap_BL - blue water footorint in m3/cap/yr
- WF_perCap_GN - green water footorint in m3/cap/yr
- WF_perCap_GY - grey water footorint in m3/cap/yr
- WF_perCap_tot - total water footorint in m3/cap/yr


Figure 10. The green, blue, grey and total water footprint of consumption per country in the period 1996-2005 (m3/yr per capita). 
	In the map showing the total water footprint of consumption per country (bottom-right), countries shown in green have a water footprint that is smaller than the global average; 
	countries shown in yellow-red have a water footprint larger than the global average.

Please cite the document as follows:

Mekonnen, M.M. and Hoekstra, A.Y. (2011) National water footprint accounts: the green, blue and grey water footprint of production and consumption, 
	Value of Water Research Report Series No. 50, UNESCO-IHE, Delft, the Netherlands.
