And let's face it: not everyone will or can stop eating meat.

Foods with meat and dairy are often the cheapest and easiest options.


And many people in this country simply cannot afford the time or cost of preparing fresh, meatless meals.



But why is this food so cheap?


One big reason: food subsidies.


The meat and dairy industries, as well as feed crops, receive the majority of food subsidies in the United States.

This allows these businesses to sell meat for a much lower cost.
